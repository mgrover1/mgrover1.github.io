# Climate Change Impacts on Weather Hazards

## Relevant Papers
[The Realization of Extreme Tornadic Storm Events Under Future Anthropogenic Climate Change](https://journals.ametsoc.org/doi/full/10.1175/JCLI-D-15-0623.1)
*Trapp and Hoogewind*
- Used CMIP5 data (MIROC5, GFDL-CM3, and NCAR-CCSM4)
- Utilized both 2D and 3D fields and computed a "climate change difference" used in conjuction with NAM data for WRF input
- Data is stored locally on Keeling (U of I Atmos Department Computer)
