# Rasberry SCI Project

The idea for this project came from the AMS Annual Meeting in Austin, Texas in 2018.
Isaac Arseneau and I talked with Eliott Foust, an employee at NCAR, who compiled
the WRF on a cluster of Raspberry Pis.

## Applying for the Guild Grant
We were fortunate enough to be offered the Guild Grant where we received over $700
to fund our research endeavors. We also reached out to both the Valparaiso University
Meteorology Department, and Computer Science Department to aid in both the construction
of the clusters and the compilation of the libraries.
